Complicated world of programming

June 2026

Everything is complicated these days, which is why I feel that it is very important to simplify everything as much as possible.

In programming there are strange words, files, errors and many small details that can break everything.

As a beginner, that can be enough to stop before even starting.

AI changes that.

I can now describe what I want in normal language and get working code. I can copy it, paste it, test it, and then change the parts I care about with the help of AI.

I do not need to understand lines of code. I can look at the result and ask for changes: make it simpler, move this, remove that, rewrite this text.

Without a doubt, the benefits of AI within the programming world outweigh the negative aspects.
